What is Data Science? - GeeksforGeeksData science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to extract actionable knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. By combining statistics, machine learning, data analysis, and computer programming, it helps organizations, such as IBM and Amazon Web Services (AWS), interpret complex data to make informed decisions, predict trends, and optimize operations. It enables personalized experiences, improves efficiency, and drives innovation across industries
